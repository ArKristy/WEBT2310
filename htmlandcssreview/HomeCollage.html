Welcome to The Collage!

A place where creativity can shine

Our Story

We are a business where everyone is welcome to express the artist within them. We provide the space and the materials for anyone to try any hobby they desire. We also have a bar to provide drinks and snacks while you indulge in whatever hobby of your choosing. Come get creative!

About

With a subscription of $25 a month, you can come in from 8am - 8pm and try out any artsy medium of your choosing. All ages welcome until 5pm when it is adults only! We have always dreamt of somewhere to store and attempt any hobby without having to buy them and store them. No more discovering your dislike of something and having to decide what to do with the rest of the materials! We got you.

History

As a child, I have always wanted to try EVERYTHING. Well, almost everything. My poor mother having to find the funds and the space for all of the hobbies I wanted, I'm sure it became exhausting. As I grew older, I realized I am not alone! Several people want to attempt many different hobbies but don't have the resources to do so. That's why we created this business for those who maybe have ADHD like I do, or just have several interests without enough time, funds, or space. Additionally, art with a little drink to sip on while surrounded by friends doing their thing is such a wonderful experience!
